*for Linda*

<p class="poetry">Blue as the ocean swells
Blue like sky above
Blue of abalone shells 
Are the eyes I love.

Blue as forget-me-nots
Blue the tears they cry
Blue of flames that hottest burn
Are those eyes divine.</p>
