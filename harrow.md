Harrow is a persona that I created to capture my [AI collaborations](ai-collab) that have a country/folk style. Harrow is a gravelly baritone who sings with acoustic guitar and not much else. The vibe I'm going for is honest songs about heartache, hope, and being human — for people who know tears can be sacred.
